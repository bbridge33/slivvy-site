FAQ
Got questions? We've got answers.
Is this a timeshare? +

Absolutely not. You receive a real legal ownership stake — documented, titled, and yours. Timeshares are usage licenses with no resale value. Slivvy is the opposite: you own it, you can sell it, you're in control.

How does scheduling work? +

Every owner gets a guaranteed allocation of days based on their share size. You book through the Slivvy app. Peak dates are allocated via a rotating priority system — fair for everyone.

What if I want to sell my share? +

List your ownership stake on the Slivvy resale marketplace anytime. We facilitate the transaction and legal transfer for a small fee. Most shares resell at or above purchase price.

Who handles maintenance and repairs? +

Slivvy manages all maintenance, repairs, housekeeping, and insurance. Costs are split equally among co-owners and billed as a transparent annual management fee — no surprise invoices.

Where are you launching first? +

Our pilot assets will be in Florida (Gulf Coast & Miami), Lake Tahoe, Scottsdale, and the Smoky Mountains — some of the most sought-after lifestyle destinations in the US.

Step 01
🔍 Browse Assets

Use the Slivvy platform to explore a curated collection of premium assets across all four categories. Filter by location, price range, asset type, and availability.

Each listing includes detailed photos, specs, ownership details, co-owner count, and a transparent cost breakdown so you know exactly what you're getting into before you sign.

Step 02
✍️ Buy Your Sliver

Choose your share size — from a 1/2 stake all the way down to 1/12. Each asset is structured as an LLC, and your share is a real equity interest with legal title.

Slivvy's legal team handles all documentation, title transfer, and co-owner agreements. You sign digitally and your ownership is recorded within 5 business days.

Step 03
📅 Schedule Your Time

The Slivvy app lets you book your guaranteed ownership days up to 12 months in advance. Each owner has a defined allocation of days based on their share size.

Our smart scheduling engine handles peak date conflicts using a fair rotating priority system — so everyone gets their fair share of holidays and high-demand weeks over time.

Step 04
🎉 Show Up & Enjoy

When your booking window arrives, just show up. Every Slivvy asset is prepared before your arrival — cleaned, stocked, fueled, and ready to go.

For vacation homes: groceries stocked, linens fresh, keys ready. For vehicles: delivered to your door, full tank. For boats: in the slip, captain briefed. Zero prep. All experience.

Step 05
💰 Sell Anytime

Life changes. Slivvy's built-in resale marketplace lets you list your ownership stake whenever you're ready to move on — no lock-in, no penalties.

We facilitate the transaction, legal transfer, and payment. Most shares resell at or above purchase price as asset values appreciate. A small resale fee applies.
